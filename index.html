Welcome to Aero.bw, your partner in promoting mental health awareness and providing accessible support for everyone in Botswana. At Aero.bw, we believe that mental health is just as important as physical health, and no one should face challenges alone. That's why we've developed innovative tools and resources to empower individuals on their mental health journey
